ATS Tips

What is ATS?

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are software that companies use to scan, filter, and rank resumes before human review.

How to Pass ATS
  • Use standard formatting - Avoid complex layouts, tables, or headers/footers
  • Include keywords - Match terms from the job description
  • Spell out acronyms - Use both the acronym and full term (e.g., "AI (Artificial Intelligence)")
  • Use standard section headings - Like "Experience," "Education," and "Skills"
  • Submit as PDF - For consistent formatting across devices
Pro Tip: Customize your resume for each job application by incorporating relevant keywords from the job description.
